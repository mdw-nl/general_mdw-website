Rchive is a cloud imaging archive that stores and processes DICOM images with minimal effort. It's designed to be both hassle-free and transparant from the initial upload to the eventual download.

Data Collection



  • Upload Client - no installation

  • Automated customizable pseudonymisation

  • Transparent at every stage
Data Processing



  • Supported computation and modification library

  • Add your own computations

  • Semi-automated FAIRification

Result Visualisation



  • Flexible search

  • Securely share data with collaborators

  • Web based DICOM viewer

About us

Medical Data Works was founded in the summer of 2017 by Andre Dekker, Maria Jacobs, Johan van Soest and Tim Lustberg as a spinout of MAASTRO Innovations. The company was started to help researchers put their innovative software solutions into practice.

Johan van Soest

Clinical Data Scientist

Johan recently finished his PhD in Clinical Data Science focussed on data interoperability and medical imaging. Johan is specialized in creating ontology solutions to translating source data into FAIR-data for our customers.

Tim Hendriks

Software Developer

With extensive experience developing for a radiotherapy clinic, Tim is able to create safe and reliable software for (clinical) research purposes. Tim can quickly adopt research image analysis software and transform it into robust solutions for large scale application.

Tim Lustberg

Clinical Data Scientist

Tim recently finished his PhD in Clinical Data Science focussed on Big Data and medical imaging. Tim is specialized in managing a project to create FAIR data infrastructures for our customers.

Preserving Great Ideas

A lot of research projects create innovative solutions for health care. However, when the project (and funding) ends a lot of these great solutions do not get used. And for good reason, the researcher writes software with a different intention, to perform their research. Medical Data Works specializes in adopting and adapting these solutions for broader use. To make sure the research software runs smoothly we provide support contracts

Medical Imaging

Imaging becoming more and more important. We provide support for Open Source projects to manage, analyze and store medical images. We also provide a centralized image archive to support research.

Distributed Learning

When you cannot bring the data together, bring the application to the data. We provide support for setting up a distributed learning client and we host a distributed learning server.

FAIR data

FAIR data will enable researchers to reduce rework and improve the innovation speed of data science in healthcare. We provide advice and customized software solutions to support FAIR data infrastructure
